Air Quality Analysis: A look at pollution levels

This project attempts to analyze how different gases are affecting air quality in countries worldwide. This project also attempts to analyze how different gases are affecting the United States specifically, by looking at individual states. By utilizing the data present for air quality, we can make conclusions on how to approach pollution in our environments.

Data was obtained from and open API source, openaq.org and the JS graphing library used was chart.js
